# Hy-Souls

Hy-Souls aims to bring Souls-like gameplay to Hytale through popular mechanics and gameplay loops.

**Version 0.0.5**

## Implemented

- **HUD**
  - Soul Counter

- **Bonfire**
  - Craftable at the workbench
  - Recipe: 4 Wood + 1 Iron Sword + 10 Soul Essence

- **Estus**
  - Craftable at the bonfire using Soul Essence
  - Three upgrade tiers available:

    | Tier | Effects                                      |
    |------|----------------------------------------------|
    | I    | Instant Heal I + Health Regen I + Stamina Regen I   |
    | II   | Instant Heal II + Health Regen II + Stamina Regen II |
    | III  | Instant Heal III + Health Regen III + Stamina Regen III |

- **Drop Table (Soul Essence)**
  - wildlife, critter, livestock → **1**
  - beast → **2**
  - intelligent, undead, void → **5**
  - elemental → **10**

- **Soul Essence Shop**
  - Exchange rates:
    - Cobalt    → 5
    - Thorium   → 10
    - Adamantite → 15

## Recommended Mods

- HyDifficulty
- Perfect Dodges
- Perfect Parries
- More Stamina!
